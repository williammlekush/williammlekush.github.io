About

I am a cautious optimist and disciplined pragmatist focused on creating delightful, empowering experiences. I believe in design thinking as a method for interrogating and expressing human values. My passions lie in interaction design, XR development, and craftsmanship.

As a student at NYUAD, I study Interactive Media to understand interactions with current and emerging technologies, such as physical computing, augmented reality, virtual reality, and artificial intelligence . These studies are complemented with concentrations in Psychology and Design, as a broad understanding of human behavior and the design process is key to creating meaningful experiences. Conceptually, I pursue methods of using and expanding the movement capacity of the human body.

During the semester, I work as a Wood Shop assistant, improving my ability to fabricate by practicing traditional joinery and carpentry. When not making or thinking about making, I spend time cross-training movement disciplines (currently climbing, hand-balancing, floreio, and powerlifting) and backpacking wilderness areas.
