Deliberate Negligence
March 2020

Collaborators | Mari Calderón and Luize Rieksta

Alex Villar’s Temporary Occupations poses meditations on how the infrastructure and architecture of a space enforce movement and determine spatial codes. Deliberate Negligance adapts Villar's concept to the NYUAD campus, focusing on the fall barriers found throughout the campus. Positioning ourselves in the vulnerabilities in this safety system, we interrogate such reactive spatial interventions, asking questions of purpose, effectiveness, and agency.
